Burger

Description

An all-time classic. What is better than a home made burger?

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • salt
  • 1 pound ground beef

Steps

  1. Put the egg and salt in one bowl and stir
  2. Add the ground beef and mix untill it blends
  3. Form patties and cook them
  4. Serve them
