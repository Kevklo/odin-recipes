Cookies

Description

Nothing better to accompany some tea than cookies, one of the best snacks ever

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Put the butter and sugar on one bowl and beat untill it's creamy
  2. Put an egg into the mix and keep beating
  3. On another bowl, put the flour, salt and baking soda and stir
  4. Put the flour on the mixing and mix untill it combines
  5. Add the Chocolate Chips into the mix
  6. Shape the mix into balls and put it on the fridge for half an hour
  7. Bake for 15 minutes
