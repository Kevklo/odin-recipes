Oreo Cake

Description

What is better than Oreo? Oreo cake

Ingredients

  • 22 Oreo cookies
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda

Steps

  1. Blend the cookies
  2. Put the cookies and the baking soda on a bowl
  3. Add the cup of milk and stir
  4. Put the mixing on a microwave safe container
  5. Put the mixing on the microwave for 5 minutes
